   ==================================================

The platform must support multiple clinic business models.

Add clinicType to the Clinic model.

* private
* government
* ngo

==================================================
CLINIC TYPE RULES
=================

PRIVATE CLINICS:

* Full billing enabled
* Invoices required
* Consultation fees enabled
* Pharmacy billing enabled
* Laboratory billing enabled
* Revenue analytics enabled

GOVERNMENT CLINICS:

* Billing disabled by default
* Consultations treated as free
* Medication treated as free
* Laboratory services treated as free
* Queue, pharmacy, appointments, consultations, and lab workflows must still function normally
* Revenue analytics hidden or limited

NGO / COMMUNITY CLINICS:

* Billing optional
* Admin can choose whether services are free or paid
* Admin can enable/disable billing features from settings
