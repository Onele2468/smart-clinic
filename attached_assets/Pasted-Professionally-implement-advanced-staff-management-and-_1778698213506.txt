2. SAFE STAFF REMOVAL (SOFT DELETE)
===================================

IMPORTANT:
Healthcare systems must NEVER permanently erase staff history.

Implement professional SOFT DEACTIVATION instead of hard deletion.

When a staff member is removed:

* account must NOT be permanently deleted
* historical records must remain intact
* prescriptions remain traceable
* consultations remain traceable
* audit history remains traceable
* queue history remains traceable

Instead:

* membership becomes inactive
* clinic access is revoked
* login access to that clinic is blocked
* status becomes:

  * active
  * inactive
  * suspended

Historical medical records must still display:

* doctor name
* nurse name
* pharmacist name
  even after deactivation.
