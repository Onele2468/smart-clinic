========================================
PHASE 4 — APPOINTMENTS, CONSULTATIONS & EMR
SMART CLINIC PLATFORM
========================================

This phase completes the core clinical workflow of the platform.

The goal is to create a full real-world patient journey:

Patient Registration
→ Appointment Scheduling
→ Queue Management
→ Nurse Assessment
→ Doctor Consultation
→ Prescription / Laboratory
→ Billing
→ Completion & Patient History

This phase must feel professional, stable, scalable, and production-ready.
