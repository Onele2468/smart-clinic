1. AUTOMATED CLINIC QUEUE WORKFLOW

---

Implement real workflow progression.

CURRENT:
Appointments and queues exist separately.

NEW WORKFLOW:

Appointment booked
→ Reception checks patient in
→ Patient enters "Waiting"
→ Nurse moves patient to "Nurse Assessment"
→ Nurse completes vitals
→ Patient moves to "Doctor Consultation"
→ Doctor consults patient
→ Doctor may:

* complete visit
* send to pharmacy
* send to lab
* request follow-up
  → Visit marked completed

REQUIREMENTS:

* Add queue status transitions
* Add timestamps for each stage
* Automatically track waiting time
* Track consultation duration
* Show queue history in patient profile

QUEUE STATUSES:

* waiting
* nurse_assessment
* doctor_consultation
* pharmacy
* laboratory
* completed
* cancelled
